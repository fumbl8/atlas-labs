# Predator–Prey Early Warning Dashboard

**Ecological simulation and warning-signal dashboard**

This prototype simulates predator/prey dynamics under simple stress scenarios and visualizes early-warning indicators before visible collapse or major regime shift.

## What it demonstrates

- Dynamical-systems thinking.
- Ecological stability and tipping-point literacy.
- Dashboard/report communication.
- Scenario comparison.
- Honest separation between educational indicators and field forecasting.

## Public-facing boundary

This is an educational simulation, not a validated ecological forecasting product. It does not use field data, formal uncertainty bands, expert calibration, or management review.
